Dalgona Coffee

  • Time: 5 mins
  • Serves: 1 serving
Ingredients Quantity
granulated sugar 2 tbsp.
instant coffee 2 tbsp.
cold water 2 tbsp.
ice as desired
milk as desired

Step 1: In a medium bowl, combine sugar, coffee, and water. Vigorously whisk until mixture turns silky smooth and shiny, then continue whisking until it thickens and holds its lofty, foamy shape.

Step 2: Fill a glass most of the way full with ice and milk, then dollop and swirl the whipped coffee mixture on top, mixing before drinking, if desired.

Source: Dalgona Coffee

Pancake

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 8 servings
Ingredients Quantity
all-purpose flour 1 1/2 cups
baking powder 3 1/2 tsp.
salt 1 tsp.
white sugar 1 tbsp.
milk 1 1/4 cups
egg 1
butter; melted 3 tbsp.

Step 1: In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.

Step 2: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.

Source: Good Old-Fashioned Pancakes

Dumplings

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 20 servings
Ingredients Quantity
flour 2 cups
salt 1 1/2 tsp.
baking powder 1 tsp.
vegetable oil 2 tsp.
warm water 1 cup
broth as needed
garnish (chopped chives
or parsley)		 as desired

Step 1: Combine flour, salt and baking powder. Add oil and water and mix until smooth. Add a bit more water until dumplings are soft and not at all dry.

Step 2: Bring broth to a boil and add dumplings by spoonful. Reduce heat and cook dumplings 4-5 minutes. (Do in batches if you are using a small pan.)

Step 3: Serve dumplings in broth garnished with chopped parsley or chives as desired.

Source: Easy Homemade Dumplings Recipe
